Departments

We offer comprehensive medical departments staffed by experienced specialists dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services. Our departments are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure the best possible care for all our patients.

Neurological Sciences Department

Our Neurology department provides comprehensive care for disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our team of experienced neurologists uses advanced diagnostic techniques and cutting-edge treatments to address conditions such as epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and stroke.

Brain Monitoring

Advanced EEG and neuroimaging services to monitor brain activity and detect abnormalities.

EEG Testing

Comprehensive electroencephalogram testing to evaluate brain function and diagnose seizures.

Neurological Exam

Thorough neurological examinations to assess motor skills, reflexes, and cognitive function.

Treatment Plans

Personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's specific neurological condition.

Surgical Services Department

Our Surgical department offers a wide range of surgical procedures performed by highly skilled surgeons. We specialize in minimally invasive techniques, general surgery, and complex surgical interventions. Our state-of-the-art operating rooms and recovery facilities ensure the highest standards of safety and care.

Minimally Invasive

Advanced laparoscopic and endoscopic procedures for faster recovery and minimal scarring.

Advanced Procedures

Complex surgeries including gastrointestinal, hernia, and thyroid procedures.

Safe Operations

Stringent safety protocols and experienced surgical teams for optimal outcomes.

Recovery Support

Comprehensive post-operative care and rehabilitation services for smooth recovery.

Dental Care Department

Our Dental Care department provides comprehensive oral health services for patients of all ages. From routine cleanings and preventive care to advanced cosmetic dentistry and orthodontic treatments, our experienced dental team is committed to helping you achieve and maintain a healthy, beautiful smile.

Oral Health

Comprehensive dental examinations and preventive care for optimal oral hygiene.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional dental cleaning and polishing to remove plaque and tartar buildup.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to enhance your appearance.

Orthodontics

Traditional braces and clear aligners for straightening teeth and correcting bites.

Ophthalmology Department

Our Ophthalmology department offers complete eye care services, from routine eye examinations to advanced surgical procedures. Our eye specialists diagnose and treat a wide range of eye conditions including cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. We utilize the latest technology to provide the best possible vision care.

Vision Testing

Comprehensive eye examinations to assess visual acuity and detect vision problems.

Retinal Imaging

Advanced retinal imaging to detect and monitor eye diseases early.

Laser Surgery

State-of-the-art laser procedures for cataract removal and vision correction.

Eye Care Plans

Personalized treatment plans and ongoing care for chronic eye conditions.

Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology department provides comprehensive heart and cardiovascular care. Our expert cardiologists offer advanced diagnostic services, treatment for heart diseases, and preventive cardiac care. We specialize in managing conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and hypertension with state-of-the-art cardiac technology.

Heart Monitoring

Continuous cardiac monitoring and evaluation for accurate diagnosis.

ECG Analysis

Detailed electrocardiogram testing to assess heart rhythm and function.

Blood Tests

Comprehensive blood work to evaluate cholesterol and cardiac markers.

Preventive Care

Heart health programs and lifestyle counseling for prevention.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care with advanced diagnostic tools and expert cardiologists dedicated to your cardiovascular health.
